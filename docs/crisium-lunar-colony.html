Crisium Lunar Colony

Crisium Lunar Colony, often called Crisium Colony or simply Crisium, is the most populous colony on the Moon.

Located at the eastern rim of Mare Crisium, Crisium is an agglomeration of airtight buildings comprising the largest indoor area built by humans.

It is a megacity and the cultural and financial capital of the Moon.

Crisium is governed as a corporate republic.

Notable People

Alex da Fonte (2091-2115) - Lives his entire life in the colony.
